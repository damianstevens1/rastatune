RastaTune Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

RastaTune is a guitar tuner app that uses your device microphone for live pitch detection. We designed RastaTune to work without accounts, advertising, tracking, analytics, or a backend server.

Information We Collect

RastaTune does not collect personal information. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, or payment information.

Microphone Use

RastaTune requests microphone access so it can listen to guitar notes and calculate pitch. Microphone audio is processed on your device for tuning feedback. Audio is not recorded, stored, sold, or uploaded by RastaTune.

Tracking And Advertising

RastaTune does not track you across apps or websites. RastaTune does not include third-party advertising.

Data Sharing

Because RastaTune does not collect personal data, we do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

RastaTune is a general-audience music utility. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
